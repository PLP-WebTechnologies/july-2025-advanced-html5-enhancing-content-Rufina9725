Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health is essential for overall well-being. Here are some key points:

  • Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act.
  • Good mental health helps us cope with stress and build relationships.
  • Mental illnesses are common but treatable conditions.
  • Seeking help early improves recovery outcomes.
